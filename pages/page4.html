BLUE FLAME

Blue Flame was Astro’s 6th mini album which was released on November 20, 2019, through Fantagio Music. Blue Flame has the same title track name which tells about one’s passionate love by comparing it to a blue flame. Astro makes diiferent genre in this era and it was so great! This is my 2nd favorite album, once you heard it definitely all day, "Ayayayayayuya~"
