Astro's Album

RISE UP

Rise Up was released on July 24, 2018 by Fantagio Music as Astro’s second special album. It depicts Astro’s dream of flying higher and farther with their fans. The special album contains five tracks together with the title track “Always You” which is a song that was dedicated to their fandom Aroha. Also, one of my favorites, the aesthetics, vibes, and Rocky's vocals! I was shocked that the main vocals here is him. But overall it was so great.
