Astro's Album

ALL LIGHT

All light was released on January 16, 2019, through Fantagio Music as their 1st full-length album. The album incorporate ASTRO’s wish to shine forever in the ‘Eternal Garden’ where the light never sets. The main music single from the album is “All Night” which tells the story of a man who expresses his feelings while waiting for his lover to contact him in the middle of the night. All light is one of my favorite albums of Astro. When I first heard 'All Night' I was stay awake all night because it is looping in my mind. The songs in this album are so calm and I really love the theme of it.
