Astro's Album

GATEWAY

Gateway is the 7th mini album by Astro. It refers to the ASTRO moving through a gateway into a new world. It was released on May 4, 2020 which contains six songs including the title song “Knock” which has a meaning of travelling through time to find someone that they will never meet in the present time and taking them to a world where they can stay together eternally. Gateway era is close to my heart; it was released at the peak of the pandemic, and these songs comfort me at that time.
